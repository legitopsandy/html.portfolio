The Best Movies According To Sandy

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

1. Thunderheart

A young mixed-blood FBI agent is assigned to work with a
cynical veteran investigator on a murder on a poverty-stricken
Sioux reservation.

2. Fast Times At Ridgemont High

The hilarious film follows a group of teenagers as they deal with
relationships, work, and school issues.

3. Meet The Fockers

Meet the Fockers is a 2004 American romantic comedy film directed
by Jay Roach and the sequel to the 2000 film Meet the Parents.
